FEATURES :

Flip

Flip Image is one of the features that you can use to edit your photos by flip the photo horizontally or vertically.

Crop

Crop Image is one of the features that you can use to edit your photos by crop the photo.

Rotate

Rotate Image is one of the features that you can use to edit your photos by rotating the photos,Enter the angle (in degrees) to rotate your image clockwise.

Blend

Rotate Image is one of the features that you can use to edit your photos by Blend the photos.
